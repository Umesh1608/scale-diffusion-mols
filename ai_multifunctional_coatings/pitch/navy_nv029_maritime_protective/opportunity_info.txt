KEY DIFFERENTIATORS FOR THIS TOPIC
================================================================================

1. GENERATIVE (not just optimization): Our platform creates NOVEL molecules
   that do not exist in any database, accessing unexplored chemistry space
   for maritime protective materials.

2. MULTIFUNCTIONAL BY DESIGN: Unlike current approaches that layer independent
   solutions, our AI co-optimizes UV, corrosion, hydrophobicity, self-healing,
   and mechanical properties in a single molecular/formulation design step.

3. CONSTRAINT-AWARE: DoD-specific constraints (PFAS-free, MIL-spec performance
   floors, VOC limits, processing temperature) are enforced DURING optimization,
   not filtered post hoc.

4. NAVY-RELEVANT VALIDATION: Computational validation against Navy-standard
   protocols (ASTM B117, G154, MIL-DTL-24441) with Phase II path to NSWCCD
   Key West Marine Corrosion Facility testing.

5. DUAL-USE POTENTIAL: Same platform applies to commercial marine coatings
   ($12.3B market), offshore wind, and coastal infrastructure — strong
   commercialization path beyond Navy applications.
